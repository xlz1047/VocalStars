VocalSet: A Singing Voice Dataset for Machine Learning
=======================================================

The WAV files in samples/reference_vocals/vocalset/ are excerpts from the
VocalSet dataset, used here as preloaded reference tracks for the VocalStars
vocal coaching app.

License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
Source:  https://zenodo.org/record/1193957
Authors: Julia Wilkins, Prem Seetharaman, Anna Huber, Juan Pablo Bello
Paper:   "VocalSet: A Singing Voice Dataset" (ISMIR 2018)

Files included:
  f1_long_forte_a.wav         — Female singer 1, forte (loud) long tone, vowel "a"
  f1_long_pp_a.wav            — Female singer 1, pianissimo (soft) long tone, vowel "a"
  f1_long_straight_a.wav      — Female singer 1, straight tone, vowel "a"
  f1_arpeggios_vibrato_u.wav  — Female singer 1, vibrato on arpeggios, vowel "u"
  f1_arpeggios_breathy_a.wav  — Female singer 1, breathy tone on arpeggios, vowel "a"
  m1_long_forte_a.wav         — Male singer 1, forte (loud) long tone, vowel "a"
  m1_long_pp_a.wav            — Male singer 1, pianissimo (soft) long tone, vowel "a"
  m1_arpeggios_vibrato_u.wav  — Male singer 1, vibrato on arpeggios, vowel "u"

These clips are used as reference audio only. No modification has been made
to the original recordings.
